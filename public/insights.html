Ready For Whatever The Economic Road Brings

The All-Terrain Portfolio was created to exist within the narrow optima of the global portfolio design space. By leveraging the concept of risk parity, the strategy seeks to deliver robust risk-adjusted returns in any economic environment. Despite its theoretical sophistication, the portfolio is incredibly simple to implement. An investor domiciled in Canada would allocate their tax-sheltered capital as follows:

  • 50% XGGB - iShares Global Government Bond Index ETF
  • 10% QTIP - Mackenzie US TIPS Index ETF (CAD-Hedged)
  • 10% CCOM - CI Auspice Broad Commodity Fund
  • 10% CEF - Sprott Physical Gold and Silver Trust
  • 10% ETC - Evolve Cryptocurrencies ETF
  • 10% CBIL - Global X 0-3 Month T-Bill ETF
