Ready For Whatever The Economic Road Brings

The All-Terrain Portfolio was created to exist within the narrow optima of the global portfolio design space. By leveraging the concept of risk parity, the strategy seeks to deliver robust risk-adjusted returns in any economic environment. Despite its theoretical sophistication, the portfolio is incredibly simple to implement. An investor domiciled in Canada would allocate their capital as follows:

  • 75% FCNS - Fidelity All-in-One Conservative ETF
  • 7.5% CCOM - CI Auspice Broad Commodity Fund
  • 7.5% CEF - Sprott Physical Gold and Silver Trust
  • 7.5% ETC - Evolve Cryptocurrencies ETF
  • 2.5% ZFS - BMO Short Federal Bond Index ETF
