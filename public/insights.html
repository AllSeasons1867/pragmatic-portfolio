Pragmatism in Practice

The Pragmatic Portfolio was created to exist within the narrow optima of the global portfolio design space. The strategy seeks to deliver the highest aggregate utility when measured upon the three metrics of simplicity, risk-adjusted return, and diversification. To implement this stategy, an investor domiciled in Canada would allocated their capital as follows:

  • 50% Vanguard Conservative Income ETF Portfolio (VCIP)
  • 10% Vanguard Global Value Factor ETF (VVL)
  • 10% Vanguard Global Momentum Factor ETF (VMO)
  • 15% CI Auspice Broad Commodity Fund (CCOM)
  • 5% Sprott Physical Gold and Silver Trust (CEF)
  • 5% Evolve Cryptocurrencies ETF (ETC)
  • 5% Vanguard Canadian Ultra-Short Government Bond Index ETF (VVSG)
